Wheels For Life

Dr. Jeremiah Kariuki started Wheels for Life, a free ambulance service for mothers in labour after dark. The program has received more than 5,000 calls and delivered around 600 babies. Patients call '1196.' A worker at a call center checks if it is an emergency. If not, they send a taxi with an overnight movement pass.
